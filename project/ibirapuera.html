Ibirapuera Park: The Green Heart of São Paulo

Welcome to Ibirapuera Park!

Often compared to New York's Central Park, Ibirapuera is a vast urban oasis, a vibrant hub of nature, culture, and recreation. On this page, you'll find a complete guide to exploring its museums, gardens, and bike paths. Discover the best spots for a picnic, the most iconic architectural landmarks, and tips to make your visit unforgettable.

Whether you're here to jog, visit a world-class museum, or simply relax by the lake, let's explore the wonders of Ibirapuera together!

Don't Miss

  • Museum of Modern Art (MAM)
  • Japanese Pavilion

Culture and Architecture

Ibirapuera is as much an architectural park as it is a natural one. Most of its iconic buildings, including the Oca (pictured), the Bienal Pavilion, and the Ibirapuera Auditorium, were designed by the legendary architect Oscar Niemeyer. The park is home to several important museums. The Afro Brasil Museum offers a deep dive into the cultural and historical contributions of African people in Brazil. The Museum of Modern Art (MAM) features a stunning collection of Brazilian art and a renowned sculpture garden.

Activities and Leisure

The park is the perfect place for outdoor activities. You can rent a bike and explore the extensive cycle paths that wind through the gardens and around the lakes. There are also designated areas for skateboarding, rollerblading, and jogging. On weekends, the park comes alive with free yoga classes, capoeira circles, and live music. For a more tranquil experience, visit the serene Japanese Pavilion, a peaceful oasis with a beautiful koi pond, or simply find a spot on the grass for a relaxing picnic.

Visitor Information

Opening Hours: The park is open daily from 5:00 AM to midnight.
Entrance: Free for all visitors.
Getting There: The closest metro stations are AACD-Servidor (Line 5 - Lilac) and Paraíso (Lines 1-Blue and 2-Green). Several bus lines also stop near the park's gates.
Safety: While generally safe, always be aware of your surroundings, especially after dark. Avoid leaving your belongings unattended.
